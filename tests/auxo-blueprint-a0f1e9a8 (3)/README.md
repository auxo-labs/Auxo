# Scope3-Pulse: Your Audit-Ready Sustainability Data Pack

## Section 1: Product Thesis & Vision
Scope3-Pulse is an automated, single-utility calculation ledger designed to empower small-to-medium suppliers. Its core vision is to dramatically simplify the complex, often intimidating process of generating audit-ready sustainability information packets, enabling suppliers to effortlessly satisfy stringent corporate compliance demands from their enterprise buyers.

This product exists to solve a critical, immediate pain point: the threat of paused vendor contracts for non-compliance with evolving sustainability regulations. By providing a secure document parser endpoint that extracts fuel/electricity usages, runs them through official EFRAG risk-based conversion tables, and spits out a compliant, single-page "VSME+ Data Pack," Scope3-Pulse allows suppliers to quickly and affordably meet reporting obligations, bypassing the need for expensive, complex enterprise carbon accounting solutions.

## Section 2: Core Functional Pillars
The MVP of Scope3-Pulse is built upon these foundational pillars to ensure focused development and rapid market validation:

1.  **Secure Document Parsing Engine:** A robust, stateless endpoint capable of ingesting raw CSV utility/shipping logs or unorganized inventory records, intelligently extracting relevant usage data.
2.  **EFRAG-Compliant Conversion:** Automated processing of extracted data against the official 2026 EFRAG risk-based conversion tables to calculate accurate, audit-ready emission values. This strictly adheres to the simplified ESRS framework and includes the "Estimate Liability Loophole" disclaimer.
3.  **Audit-Ready VSME+ Data Pack Generation:** Production of a single-page, signable PDF or JSON compliance manifest designed specifically for corporate compliance officers, ensuring immediate utility and acceptance.
4.  **Transactional Monetization Model:** Implementation of a "Pay-Per-Report" system, offering immediate value to suppliers needing quick compliance without recurring software overhead.

## Section 3: Ubiquitous Domain Vocabulary
To maintain consistency and clarity across the codebase and team communications, the following terms are standardized:

| Human Term                          | Code Property (camelCase)         | Context Definition                                                                                              |
| :---------------------------------- | :-------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| VSME+ Data Pack                     | `vsmeDataPack`                    | The single-page, audit-ready sustainability compliance manifest generated by the application.                   |
| EFRAG Conversion Tables             | `efragConversionTables`           | The official risk-based conversion factors used to transform raw data into emission values.                     |
| Utility Log                         | `utilityLog`                      | A CSV or text file containing records of electricity, gas, or other utility consumption.                        |
| Shipping Log                        | `shippingLog`                     | A CSV or text file detailing goods movement, including fuel consumption, distances, or weights.                  |
| Emission Value                      | `emissionValue`                   | The calculated amount of greenhouse gas emissions (e.g., CO2e) based on EFRAG standards.                        |
| Corporate Compliance Demands        | `corporateComplianceDemands`      | The sustainability reporting requirements imposed by large enterprise buyers on their suppliers.                |
| Double Materiality                  | `doubleMateriality`               | The principle requiring companies to report on both financial materiality and impact materiality.               |
| Value-Chain Cap                     | `valueChainCap`                   | Regulatory limits or scope definitions applied to sustainability reporting across the supply chain.             |
| Secondary Value-Chain Proxies       | `secondaryValueChainProxies`      | Estimation methods used in lieu of direct measurements for calculating emissions, as allowed by EFRAG.         |
| Compliance Manifest                 | `complianceManifest`              | The final output document (PDF/JSON) that officially states a supplier's sustainability data.                   |
| Transactional Report Run            | `transactionalReportRun`          | A single instance of generating a VSME+ Data Pack, subject to the pay-per-report model.                         |
| Omnibus Directive                   | `omnibusDirective`                | The overarching European directive governing corporate sustainability reporting.                                |
| ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards) | `esrsStandards`      | The simplified reporting framework for small-to-medium enterprises under EFRAG.                                 |
